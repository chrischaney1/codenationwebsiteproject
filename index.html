Welcome to the Twisted Emporium UK.
Custom Art and Furniture.

Formed in 2018, The Twisted Emporium is a creative company based in Redditch specialising in Custom Art pieces, Furniture revamping and Quirky handmade household items.

We are able to completely revitalise any item of furniture you may have and also create one of a kind artworks to compliment your projects.

We offer full Mural design and painting services which enables you to carry your theme across several pieces of furniture, walls and framed highlight pieces.
